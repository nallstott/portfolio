ACDelco

Role Art Director / Designer

For ACDelco, the official parts brand of General Motors alongside GM Genuine Parts, my writer and I were tasked with coming up with some more engaging social concepts for the brand. We tried to create experiences that captured more of an interactive, gamified experience of IG stories than typical photography. I also worked on "Art of the Part", a series where several teams play with different styles to make auto parts come alive.
