Role Art Director / Designer / Product Designer / Founder / CEO

After a while working in retail, I decided I wanted to it myself. Seasons 1 and 2 were graphic prints, 3 was cut-and-sew by yours truly, and 4 brings my art to my love of skating. I did everything but the photos for this project.

Season 1

When I started Season 1, I didn't even know what CMYK meant. I was lucky enough that my roommate's brother had a screenprinting business and some cheap Gildan blanks.

Season 2

Upon discovering Threadless' new Artist Shops, I was finally able to make whatever I wanted, regardless of any production limitations.

Season 3

With some great help, I learned patternmaking and sewing. I designed a few shirt silhouettes and made a collection of unisex basics alongside custom denim pieces.

Season 4

Returning to my love of graphics, I created a series of skate decks. And yes, you can skate them.
