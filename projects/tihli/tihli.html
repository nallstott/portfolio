TIHLI
Skincare

Role Art Director / Designer

The creator of the brand came to us with nothing more than a product. At FIR NW, the student ad agency at Portland State University, we gave the product a name, identity, and a face so it could live in the real world. I oversaw the art side of the project and designed the packaging and website.

Packaging

Inspired by a box of film slides my roommate had, I wanted to create packaging that visually fit in within the beauty space, was affordable to produce, and was something outside the traditional box packaging.

Brand Guidelines

Putting the brand's spirit on paper. Client's likeness removed at their request.

Website Walkthrough

Creating a digital home for the D2C brand.
